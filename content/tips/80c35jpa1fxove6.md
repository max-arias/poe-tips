<p>Paste this into vendors to instantly highlight links (R-R-G, G-G-R) and movement speed boots: `-\w-.-|b-b-b|g-g-r|g-r-g|r-g-g|nne|rint|ms`.&nbsp;</p>
<p>**Usage:**<br>1. Copy text.<br>2. Open Vendor window.<br>3. Paste in search bar.</p>
