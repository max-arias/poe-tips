### **The Scarab Shuffle (Vendor Recipe)**
Buy the cheapest, most useless scarabs in bulk and recycle them into valuable ones.
* **Buy Bulk Trash:** Use the trade site to buy thousands of the cheapest scarabs (e.g., 60-100 per Divine).
* **Vendor Them:** Sell 3 Scarabs to any vendor to receive 1 new random Scarab.
* **Filter & Sell:** Use a regex in your stash to highlight valuable scarabs (e.g., Ambush, Divination, Horned Scarabs).
* **Repeat:** Vendor the mid-tier/trash outcomes again until you are left with only high-value scarabs to sell for profit.

### **Bulk Flipping (The Convenience Tax)**
Buy items one by one and sell them in large stacks to players who will pay extra to save time.
* **Pick a Commodity:** Choose high-volume items like Essences, Fossils, Catalyst, or Oils.
* **Set Buy Orders:** List a "Chaos Orb" for "Essence of Woe" trade on the official site to buy them passively one by one.
* **Mark Up:** Once you have a stack (e.g., 50+), relist them for 20-40% more than you paid.
* **Sell:** Wait for a "whale" crafter to buy your entire stock in one trade.

### **Flask Rolling (The Alt-Spam)**
Take high-item-level flask bases and craft them into "perfect" utility flasks for popular builds.
* **Get Bases:** Buy ilvl 84+ Utility Flasks (Quicksilver, Granite, Diamond, etc.) with 20% Quality (or Glassblower them yourself).
* **Transmute & Alt:** Use Orb of Transmutation and Alteration Orbs to hit Tier 1 prefixes (e.g., "Alchemist's", "Abecedarian's").
* **Augment:** Use an Orb of Augmentation to hit a good suffix (e.g., Attack Speed, Cast Speed, Armor).
* **Automate:** Go to your crafting bench and add "Used when Charges reach full" (costs Instilling Orbs and Glassblower's Baubles).
* **Sell:** List the finished flask for 100c - 1 Divine depending on the roll.

### **Cluster Jewel Crafting**
Buy empty Cluster Jewel bases and craft them into 2-3 notable setups.
* **Identify Meta:** Check PoE Ninja to see which Large Cluster Jewels are most used (e.g., 8-passive Bow Damage, Chaos Damage, or Minion Damage).
* **Buy Bases:** Buy the base item level 50-75 (check specifically which ilvl is needed for the notables to block bad mods).
* **Fossil/Harvest Spam:** Use Dense/Pristine fossils or Harvest "Reforge" options to force specific tags.
* **Profit:** Stop when you hit 3 good notables and 2 jewel sockets, then sell.

### **Divination Card Arbitrage**
Buy partial stacks or full sets of cards that result in a profit when turned in or sold as the item.
* **Find the Gap:** Look for cards that reward unique items (e.g., *The Nurse* for Headhunter, or cards for Watcher's Eyes).
* **Calculate:** Check if the cost of the full set is significantly lower than the cost of the resulting item.
* **Turn In:** Buy the cards, turn them in to Tasuni/Lilly, and sell the resulting item.
* **Gamble (Optional):** Buy "unidentified" item cards (like for Watcher's Eye), turn them in, identify, and sell if the rolls are good (high risk).

### **Prophecy/Memory Flipping (Modern Equivalent)**
Buy "Memory" lines or specific itemized mechanics that people are too lazy to run or don't understand.
* **Einhar's Memories:** Buy specific memories (e.g., *Harvest Beasts* or *Essences*) that are underpriced.
* **Resell or Run:** If you have a runner, run them; if not, re-list them at the "bulk" price if you can corner a small part of the market.
* **Voidborne Keys:** Buy *Voidborne Reliquary Keys* when they dip in price and resell them when streamers open them, driving the price up (market timing).

### **Double Corrupting Uniques**
Buy popular uniques, maximize their quality, and risk them for massive payouts.
* **Buy Gems/Items:** Buy popular gems (e.g., Enlighten, Empower) or uniques (e.g., Lightning Coil, Cloak of Flame).
* **Temple Flip:** Buy *Alva's Memory of Reverse Incursion* or "Locus of Corruption" chronicles.
* **Poof or Profit:** Use the altar. If it hits a good "Double Corrupt" implicit, the item value can go from 1 Divine to 50+ Divines.

### **The "Lazy Exile" Kingsmarch Strat**
*From your uploaded files. This is the only true hideout strategy in those documents; the others (Abyss/Breach) require mapping.*

**Crop Shipping (The Divine Printer)**
Ship specific crops to Kalguur for raw Divine Orbs.
* **Hire:** Rank 9 or 10 Merchants and Sailors.
* **Load:** 10,000 Blue Zanthimum.
* **Boost:** Add 240,000 Dust (optional, but recommended in your files).
* **Send:** Ship to **Kalguur**.
* **Repeat:** Do this constantly for a ~60-70% chance of Divine Orbs per ship.

**The Mirror Lottery (High Value)**
Ship massive crop values to fish for Mirror Shards.
* **Stockpile:** Grow millions of Wheat and Corn.
* **Load:** Mix Wheat and Corn until Shipment Value hits **17 Million** (approx 315k of each crop + dust to fill).
* **Send:** Ship to **Ngakanu** or **Riben Fell**.
* **Goal:** Mirror Shards and Power Runes.

**Dust Arbitrage**
Buy cheap uniques and disenchant them for profit.
* **Search:** Look for high-dust uniques like *Betrayal's Sting*, *Yoke of Suffering*, or *Malachai's Mark*.
* **Quality:** Use Whetstones/Scraps to hit 20% Quality (massively increases dust).
* **Disenchant:** Dump them in the Kingsmarch Disenchanter.
* **Use:** Use this dust to multiply the value of your crop shipments.

### **Community Hideout Strategies**
*Sourced from current forums and Reddit.*

**The Bauble Flip**
Profit from the "Glassblower's Bauble" vendor recipe changes.
* **Buy:** Buy high-level Utility Flasks with 0% quality in bulk.
* **Quality:** Use Glassblower's Baubles to hit 20% quality.
* **Vendor:** Sell the 20% quality flask to a vendor.
* **Note:** In 3.25, quality on flasks adds player power, making Baubles expensive. Check the market price of Baubles vs. the cost of the base flask to see if the margin exists. *Correction: The classic recipe (quality flasks -> baubles) was removed/changed in recent patches; verify the current 3.25 vendor exchange rates first.*

**Rog "The pog" Crafting**
Use Expedition currency to craft items without leaving your stash.
* **Buy:** Buy *Tujen* or *Rog* Logbooks (or just the reroll currency: *Scrap Metal* / *Artifacts*).
* **Craft:** Spam Rog. Look for "Suppression," "Chaos Res," and "T1 Life" on boots/gloves/helmets.
* **Take:** Always take the "Upgrade modifier tier" options.
* **Sell:** List anything with 3x T1/T2 suffixes for 1-5 Divines.
