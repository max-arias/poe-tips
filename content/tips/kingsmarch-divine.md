### **Fast Rotation for Raw Currency**

* **Load:** Load exactly **10,000 Blue Zanthimum** onto a ship (Dust is optional, but ~240-2000 dust can help).
* **Target:** Send the shipment to **Kalguur**.
* **Why:** This small shipment size breaks certain breakpoints to frequently return **1-3 Divine Orbs**.
* **Speed:** Unlike the "Mirror Lottery" (which requires days of stockpiling), you can run this constantly throughout the day for steady income.
