### **Snipe Cheap Dust**

* **Analyze:** Go to **[WealthyExile.com/dust](https://wealthyexile.com/dust)** to see a live-updated list of unique items sorted by **Dust per Chaos**.
* **Target:** Look for items offering **>20,000 Dust per Chaos**. Common winners are often *Voltaxic Rift*, *Bino's Kitchen Knife*, or *Arakaali's Fang*.
* **Quality:** ALWAYS apply **20% Quality** (Whetstones/Scraps) before disenchanting. This multiplies the dust yield significantly for almost zero cost (e.g., turning 300k dust into 360k+).
* **Ilvl 84+:** Strictly buy items that are **Item Level 84 or higher**. Below this threshold, the base dust value drops by a hidden multiplier, ruining your margins.
