### **Smash & Pray**

* **Isolate Mods:** Alt-spam **Ezomyte Tower Shields** (ilvl 84+) to isolate "Life on Block" on one and "Reservation Efficiency" on another.
* **Merge:** Use a **Recombinator** to combine them, aiming to keep both suffixes.
* **Finish:** Lock suffixes (*Wild Bristle Matron*) and Harvest Reforge Life.
