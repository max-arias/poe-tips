### **Expedition Flipping**

* **Farm Rog:** Refresh Rog’s inventory using artifacts.
* **Craft:** Look for Evasion bases (Boots/Gloves) and prioritize "Upgrade Modifier Tier."
* **Sell:** List items with **T1 Spell Suppression** + **Life** for 2+ Divines.
