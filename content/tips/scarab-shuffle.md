### **Vendor Trash for Profit**

* **Generate Regex:** Go to [poe.re/scarab](https://poe.re/#/scarab), select the cheapest scarabs (under 1c), and copy the generated regex string.
* **Filter Trade:** Paste this regex into the trade site or your stash to instantly highlight only the "trash" scarabs you want to buy or vendor.
* **Vendor:** Sell **3 Scarabs** of the same type to a vendor to receive **1 Random Scarab** in return.
* **Profit:** Keep high-value outcomes (Ambush, Divination) and re-vendor the rest.
