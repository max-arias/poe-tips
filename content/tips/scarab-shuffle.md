### **Maximize Profit Without Typing**

* **Generate Regex:** Go to [poe.re/scarab](https://poe.re/#/scarab), select the cheapest scarabs (under 1c), and copy the generated regex string.
* **Dual Open:** Stand between Stash and Faustus. Open Stash, then hold `Ctrl` and click Faustus to overlap the UI.
* **The Swap:** **Ctrl + Click** a scarab to load it, then **Ctrl + Click** the **Exchange Arrow** (between "I Have/Want") to flip the trade direction.
* **Auto-Price:** Delete the value in the **Price Field** (Chaos/Divine). The UI will automatically fill in the best current "Ask" price for your specific stack size.
* **List:** **Ctrl + Click** the arrow again to flip back to "Selling" mode, then click **Place Order**.
* **Result:** You are now listed at the competitive market rate (selling for more) rather than dumping instantly into a Buy order (selling for less).
