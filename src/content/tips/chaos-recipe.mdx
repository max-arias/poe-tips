## How It Works

Selling a **full set** of Rare (yellow) items to any NPC vendor will reward you with Chaos Orbs. To maximize profit, items should be kept **unidentified**.

## The Full Set Requirements

To complete the recipe, you must sell one of each of the following in a single transaction:

* **Helmet** (1)
* **Body Armour** (1)
* **Gloves** (1)
* **Boots** (1)
* **Belt** (1)
* **Amulet** (1)
* **Rings** (2)
* **Weapon** (One 2-Handed weapon **OR** two 1-Handed weapons)

## Important Rules

1.  **Item Level:** All items must be **Item Level 60 to 74**. If any item is below iLvl 60, you will receive a Chance Orb. If all items are iLvl 75+, you will receive a Regal Orb.
2.  **Unidentified Bonus:** If the entire set is **unidentified**, the vendor gives you **2 Chaos Orbs**. If even one item is identified, you only receive **1 Chaos Orb**.

## Strategic Tips

* **The Bottleneck:** Rings, Amulets, and Belts drop much less frequently than armor. Only pick up enough armor/weapons to match your jewelry count to save stash space.
* **The "One High Level" Trick:** If you have mostly iLvl 75+ items (Regal recipe), you can include **just one** item between iLvl 60-74 to force the vendor to give you Chaos Orbs instead.
* **Ignore Bases:** Don't worry about the stats or the base type of the items. Since you aren't identifying them, a "bad" base is worth exactly the same as a "good" base.
