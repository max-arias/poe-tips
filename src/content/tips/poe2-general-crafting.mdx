## The Core Essentials

If you plan on crafting endgame gear, these items are non-negotiable for the "Foundation and Manipulation" phases[cite: 3, 80, 157].

- **Greater Essences:** Used to turn magic bases into rare items with a guaranteed second Tier 1 stat[cite: 8, 82, 160].
  - *Priority:* **Abrasion** (Physical), **Enchantment/Enhancement** (Defenses), and **Sorcery** (Spells)[cite: 8, 79, 165].
- **Exalted Orbs & Greater Exalted Orbs:** Standard Exalts add suffixes for crystallization bait, while Greater Exalts ensure you skip low-tier (T4-T5) rolls[cite: 10, 87, 150].
- **Omens of Dextral Crystallization:** The single most important tool for deterministically swapping junk suffixes for build-defining mods like **+5 Skill Levels** or **Onslaught**[cite: 77, 88, 90].

## The "Well of Souls" Kit

These items are required for the final "Finisher" phase to safely add a 6th Tier 1 modifier[cite: 12, 127, 163].

- **Preservation Items:** Used to protect the item and access the Well of Souls[cite: 12, 127, 163].
  - **Preserved Jawbone:** For Weapons[cite: 67, 127].
  - **Preserved Grip/Rib:** For Body Armour and Boots[cite: 12, 31, 32].
  - **Ancient/Preserved Collarbone:** For Rings and Amulets[cite: 109].
- **Abyssal Echoes Omen:** Highly recommended for every high-end craft; it doubles your choices in the Well of Souls[cite: 12, 133].
- **Necromancy Omens:**
  - **Dextral Necromancy:** Forces the Well of Souls to offer only **Suffixes**[cite: 73, 128].
  - **Sinistral Necromancy:** Forces the Well of Souls to offer only **Prefixes**[cite: 129, 164].
